About us

We,Ghazal and Nahal launched cake and Cookies on July 6,2012. With the help of our dedicated team of bakers, decorators, and customer service staff, Dipon now offers a variety of freshly baked cookies, pastries, cupcakes wedding cakes, and specialty cakes.

Dipon isn't your typical bakery. If your'e the type who wants fresh, home-made goodness, we make that happen every day here at Dipon along with some unique options which you won't find at your superstore bakery. we bake fresh home-made cookies for your enjoyment. If you are looking to order something special, our customer service staff is ready to help you choose that unique gift or party centerpiece you are looking for!.

The most special part of Dipon is our team of wonderful staff both in the kitchen and behind the counter. We love to interact with our regular customers as well as the treat of introducing a first-time visitor to our bakery. If you have ordered a custom cake from us, the chances are good that we will remember it! Whether it was for a child's birthday, parent's anniversary, graduation or a wedding, we often recall much about the creation of the cake, even down to the experience of delivering and setting up at weddings as well! Our staff takes such pride in what they do each day! Whether they are baking and frosting cakes or cookies, preparing icings and fillings, or boxing up goodies for customers in line. no detail is overlooked. We enjoy taking care of business in every way as a team at Dipon ! Add in our fantastic customers and it is truly a great place to be!

Come visit us in the Shopping Center plaza cote des neiges and treat yourself to the best cookies and cakes in town!

Our dedication to producing one-of-a-kind cakes, cookies, and desserts using only the freshest and finest ingredients has resulted in a thriving business, and has established Dipon Cookies and Cakes as the most popular dessert shop in Montreal. We would not have been able to accomplish everything without our amazing and talented team.
